# AI Agent Use Case Directory (500+ Projects)

Back to [[GitHub Library]].

## What it is

A curated, community-maintained directory of 500+ real AI agent implementations, organised by industry and by framework. 31.6k stars, 5.5k forks. Each entry links directly to a working open-source repo or notebook.

- **GitHub:** https://github.com/ashishpatel26/500-AI-Agents-Projects
- **Status:** shelf (reference and inspiration, not something to install)
- **Captured:** 2026-06-25

## Why it matters for Orwell

The Orwell north star is businesses 80% staffed by agents at a tenth of human cost. This repo is essentially a map of what is already being built and where the proven patterns are. Two direct uses:

1. **Idea validation.** Before designing a new soul or department, check here for a working reference implementation in the same domain.
2. **New business identification.** The industry coverage is wide (healthcare, finance, retail, logistics, legal, HR, real estate, energy). Any sector with multiple working agent implementations is a viable Orwell vertical.

## Coverage by industry

Healthcare (diagnostics, insurance claims), Finance (trading bots, stock analysis, financial reasoning), Education (tutoring, study partners, research), Customer service (24/7 chatbots), Retail and e-commerce (product recommendation, personal shopping), Supply chain and logistics (route optimisation), Manufacturing (process monitoring), Real estate (property pricing), Legal (document review), HR and recruitment (candidate matching, job posting generation), Cybersecurity (threat detection, red-team testing), Energy (demand forecasting), Entertainment and media (content personalisation, trend analysis), Gaming.
