# Priority system

A grading scheme so we always know what matters and what to ignore. The point is focus: keep the number of P1s small and be honest about the rest.

## Grades

- **P1 - Drive now.** Directly earns revenue or moves the north star this quarter. Gets active build time. Cap P1 at roughly four. If everything is P1, nothing is.
- **P2 - Scheduled.** Important, will get attention, but not this week's fight. Maintained, not pushed.
- **P3 - Parked or someday.** Logged, not worked. Revisit at the grading review. A candidate to cut.

## Status (separate from grade)

- **active** - being worked now
- **maintenance** - kept running, not developed
- **stalled** - meant to be moving but is not
- **parked** - deliberately on hold

The dangerous combination is P1 + stalled. The grading review exists to catch it.

## Cadence

- **Daily** summary lists what was filed, decisions, and open loops.
- **Weekly** review summarises what moved and what stalled, ranks open loops.
- **Monthly** grading review re-grades everything, flags P1-but-stalled and low-grade time-sinks, and recommends what to push and what to drop.
