## 3. Ubisoft Barcelona strike enters day two
**Source:** [VideoGamesChronicle](https://www.videogameschronicle.com/news/assassins-creed-black-flag-resynced-studio-ubisoft-barcelona-strikes-to-protest-51-staff-being-laid-off/); [GameDeveloper.com](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/business/ubisoft-barcelona-to-go-on-strike-over-proposed-layoffs)

Today is day two of the three-day action (14-16 July) called by the CGT-affiliated union CSVI over 51 proposed layoffs. Demands are unchanged: cancel the layoffs outright, a five-year no-mass-layoff guarantee, honouring stalled promotions and pay reviews, and restoring the studio's 60%-remote work policy. Ubisoft's public position also hasn't moved — it says it's "aware of the strike," respects staff's "right to express their views," and won't decide until collective consultation concludes. No escalation today; logged as a continuation, not a fresh development, industry-colour only.
