## Story 1: Palworld leaves Early Access, 1.0 is live

**Source:** Multiple (Dot Esports, Windows Central, Xbox Wire) | https://dotesports.com/palworld/guides/palworld-1-0-release-time-countdown | https://www.windowscentral.com/gaming/palworld-rockets-onto-steams-top-10-most-played-games-list | https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/07/07/xbox-game-pass-july-2026-wave-1/
**Related vault notes:** [[Products]]

Pocketpair's Palworld 1.0 went live today as promised, closing out roughly two and a half years in Early Access. It's a free update for the 40 million-plus existing owners, no save wipe required, and ships simultaneously on PC, PS5, Xbox Series X|S and Switch 2, with day one availability on PC Game Pass and Xbox Game Pass Ultimate/Premium as part of the previously flagged July Wave 1. Headline additions confirmed in the launch build: a new flight system, weapon-wielding Pals, and an endgame region built around a giant World Tree, on top of a changelog Pocketpair itself describes as 27 PDF pages long.

Reception so far is positive but not yet fully measured. The official Palworld status page shows no incidents and stable servers today, a contrast with the game's rockier January 2024 Early Access debut when a surge in players caused widespread connection failures. Analyst expectation ahead of launch was a strong but not record-breaking concurrent spike, likely into six figures and possibly past a million on Steam alone, well short of the 2.1 million all-time peak from January 2024, on the logic that Game Pass and console availability now spread demand across platforms rather than concentrating it on Steam. Worth a proper look once player-count data settles over the weekend, purely for the survival/base-building design-pattern interest this beat tracks, not because it touches NORTHLANDS code directly.
