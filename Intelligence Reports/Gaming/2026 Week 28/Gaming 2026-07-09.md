## Story 4: Flaregate Network launches today — an RTS-inspired roguelike deckbuilder

**Source:** Steam | https://store.steampowered.com/app/3298430/Flaregate_Network/
**Related vault notes:** [[Products]]

A small but genuinely today-dated release for the sweep: Flaregate Network hit Steam on 9 July, billed as "a roguelike deckbuilder with RTS-inspired spaceship combat." The structure alternates a paused Play Phase, where the player builds a deck and plans fleet composition with no time pressure, against a real-time Battle Phase where fleets clash automatically with no further input, essentially auto-battler mechanics wrapped around deckbuilding logic rather than direct-control RTS. It ships with two campaign modes — a scripted story campaign and a procedurally randomised roguelike mode — plus a damage-type counter system (explosive vs laser) layered over unit composition. This is a niche indie release, not a AAA signal, but it's a clean example of the "RTS-adjacent" hybridisation genre trend (deckbuilder-meets-RTS, similar in spirit to Frost Giant's and other 2026 hybrid experiments) worth noting for the sweep rather than for any NORTHLANDS relevance.

## Story 5: GTA Online's 9-13 July event week goes live with Kortz Center Heist prep built in

**Source:** RockstarINTEL | https://rockstarintel.com/gta-online-event-week-new-update-preparation-july-9th-13th/
**Related vault notes:** [[Products]]

GTA Online's weekly reset (Rockstar resets bonuses every Thursday) turned over today into a shortened 9-13 July event week, the last full weekly cycle before the Kortz Center Heist lands as Title Update 1.73 on 14 July. Today's bonuses: 2x GTA$ and RP on Bunker Research and Sell Missions plus 2x Research Speed, 3x GTA$/RP on Stunt Race Series, and a Heist completion bonus of GTA$1,000,000 within 72 hours plus a free NOOSE Outfit. Routine weekly churn on its own, but the Fine Art File tie-in confirms Rockstar is sequencing the pre-heist content deliberately rather than dropping Kortz Center cold. No FiveM/NORTHLANDS relevance directly — this is GTA Online live-service content, not the base game or Enhanced transition — logged for the Rockstar-news sweep only.
