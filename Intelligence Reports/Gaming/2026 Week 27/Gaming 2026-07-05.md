**Source:** Web search cross-reference (Slashdot, Gamereactor, Hitmarker coverage of the 2024 IGN/Ziff Davis acquisition)
**Related vault notes:** [[Products]]

Fifth consecutive report with all three of the IGN games feed, Rock Paper Shotgun feed, and GamesIndustry.biz feed unreachable at fetch level. Digging into why: all three outlets (plus Eurogamer and VG247) were acquired into a single stable by IGN Entertainment/Ziff Davis back in 2024, consolidating Gamer Network's titles under one publisher.
