## Story 2: Google Business Profile reviews issue remains unresolved, no timeline

**Source:** Search Engine Land, "Google is investigating reports of reviews going missing and pausing reviews on local listings" (3 July, previously logged) | https://searchengineland.com/google-is-investigating-reports-of-reviews-going-missing-and-pausing-reviews-on-local-listings-481616 — Optimixed, "Google Business Profile Reviews Go Missing For Many" (via web search) | https://www.optimixed.com/google-business-profile-reviews-go-missing-for-many/
**Related vault notes:** [[SEO Portfolio]], [[Comparison Network]]

No movement since the 3 July confirmation. Google's position is unchanged: the company is investigating widespread review disappearances and spam-detection-triggered pausing of new reviews on local listings, and says it will restore legitimate reviews mistakenly removed, but has given no resolution timeline. Separately, worth noting for context (not a today-dated development): Google's early-2026 Prohibited and Restricted Content policy update banned asking customers to name staff in reviews and pressuring for reviews on-premises, with global enforcement — including retroactive sweeps of older reviews — expanding through May-June. That enforcement expansion is the more plausible explanation for the current wave of disappearances than a pure technical bug, though Google hasn't confirmed a direct causal link.

Any portfolio or comparison-network property with a Google Business Profile listing (pet transport, funeral repatriation, close protection, bus hire, pest control all have plausible local-listing angles) remains exposed with no way to check real exposure except by looking directly. This is the second consecutive report carrying this thread with no movement — worth the five-minute manual GBP check across exposed properties this week before it's parked.
