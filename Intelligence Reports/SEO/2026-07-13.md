## Story 1: Google Business Profile review bugs remain a live, two-part saga, no new development today

**Source:** Search Engine Journal | https://www.searchenginejournal.com/google-business-profiles-showing-empty-review-dashboards/582015/ ; Search Engine Roundtable (via web search) | https://www.seroundtable.com/google-you-have-no-reviews-yet-41662.html
**Related vault notes:** [[SEO Portfolio]], [[Comparison Network]]

No new movement today, but worth restating clearly since the two bugs are easy to conflate and both remain relevant to the portfolio's reliance on local listings. Bug one, reported from 3 July: Google's spam-detection systems stripped legitimate reviews, sometimes triggered by an owner reporting a fake review themselves; Google says this is resolved. Bug two, reported from 9 July, still generating fresh complaint threads as of the weekend: profile dashboards show "you have no reviews yet" while the public listing still shows the full review count underneath, a display-only bug rather than a removal. Google confirmed the display bug fixed on 10 July with a day or two needed for full reappearance, that window has now passed; if any portfolio or comparison-network listing is still showing the empty-dashboard state, it's worth a manual check rather than assuming it self-resolved on schedule.
