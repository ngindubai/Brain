## Story 1: Google confirms investigating vanished reviews and over-aggressive review-pausing on local listings

**Source:** Search Engine Land, "Google is investigating reports of reviews going missing and pausing reviews on local listings" (3 July) | https://searchengineland.com/google-is-investigating-reports-of-reviews-going-missing-and-pausing-reviews-on-local-listings-481616
**Related vault notes:** [[SEO Portfolio]], [[Comparison Network]]

Business owners have been reporting, over several days in early July, that customer reviews on their Google Business Profile listings are disappearing, and in some cases new reviews are being blocked entirely — with at least some ratings dropping to zero as a result. Google's stated line: "When our systems detect suspicious reviews, we take a range of actions including removing reviews and temporarily pausing reviews on the profile to prevent further abuse. We are investigating the issue and will restore any reviews that were incorrectly removed."

Read plainly, that's Google saying its own spam-detection system may be over-triggering — treating legitimate profiles as abuse targets. No resolution timeline was given, and the recommended action for affected businesses is to flag the issue in Google Business Profile community forums and wait.

Why it matters here specifically: any portfolio or comparison-network property that relies on Google Business Profile — a physical-presence angle for local lead-gen (pet transport, funeral repatriation, close protection, bus hire, pest control all have plausible local-listing components) or any comparison-network entity with a GBP listing — is exposed to this if it gets caught in the same net. There's no evidence yet that any Orwell property is affected, but this is exactly the kind of thing worth a five-minute manual check across GBP listings this week rather than waiting for a review count to visibly drop.

## Story 2: Google Search Console page indexing report restored after three-week outage

**Source:** Search Engine Land, "Google indexing report in Google Search Console fixed" (3 July) | https://searchengineland.com/google-indexing-report-in-google-search-console-fixed-481610 — Search Engine Roundtable (via web search), "Google Page Indexing Report Has Been Fixed & Updated" | https://www.seroundtable.com/google-page-indexing-report-fixed-and-updated-41626.html
**Related vault notes:** [[SEO Portfolio]], [[Comparison Network]]

The page indexing report in GSC had been frozen at 11 June data for three weeks — anyone trying to debug indexing issues across that window was working blind. As of 3 July it's updated through 29 June, and reporting normally again. This affected every property with a Search Console account, portfolio and comparison-network domains included, so anything that looked like an indexing problem over the past three weeks may simply have been the reporting lag rather than a real crawl/index issue. Worth a quick sanity pass on any domain where an indexing concern was flagged in that window, now that live data is back.
