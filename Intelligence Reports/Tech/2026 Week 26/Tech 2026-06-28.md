## Story 4: Government-coordinated model release — a new normal
**Source:** OpenAI | https://openai.com/index/previewing-gpt-5-6-sol/ | CNN | https://www.cnn.com/2026/06/26/tech/anthropic-mythos-release
**Related vault notes:** [[Agent Org]], [[Agentic AI Keyword Forecast]]

Both GPT-5.6 and Mythos restoration followed identical pattern: government pre-brief, limited partner release, broader access later. June 2 EO codified this. Frontier model releases now carry government-clearance overhead. Single-model dependency on Anthropic is a demonstrated operational risk. Practical hedge: no critical workflow should have a single-model dependency.
